About Bachelor Zone

This is a flat where bachelor students live and study.

Welcome to Bachelor Zone, the ideal living space for bachelor students in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Our flat caters to the needs of students pursuing higher education at Polytechnic or Engineering institutions, providing a comfortable and conducive environment for living and studying.

Our Story

Established on June 01, 2024, Bachelor Zone was founded with the vision of creating a tailored living space for bachelor students. We understand the unique requirements of students living away from home, and our flat is designed to meet those needs.

What We Offer

At Bachelor Zone, students can expect more than just a place to stay. Our flat offers a range of amenities to enhance the living experience, including high-speed internet, study areas, and recreational spaces. Safety and security are our top priorities, ensuring peace of mind for residents.

Our Mission

Our mission at Bachelor Zone is to provide a supportive and enriching environment for bachelor students to thrive academically and personally. We aim to foster a sense of community and belonging among residents, creating lasting memories and friendships.

Experience the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and community living at Bachelor Zone.
